# Related Posts

Shows related posts at the bottom of each post based on shared category.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Default** | Disabled |
| **Slug** | `related-posts` |

## What It Does

Shows a list of related posts below the post content, based on the same category. The number of related posts shown is configurable.

## Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| `related_count` | Number of Related Posts. How many related posts to show (1-6). |

## Activation

Enable from **Admin → Modules**. After activation, configure the settings from the module's settings page.
